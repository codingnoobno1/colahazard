# Retailer Dashboard Requirements

## Overview
A specialized dashboard for retailers to manage inventory, optimize shelf space, and track specific bottle types (Thick, Thin, rPET).

## Key Features

### 1. QR Code Generation
- **Functionality**: Generate QR codes for specific racks or product batches.
- **Use Case**: Quick scanning for inventory updates and sales tracking.

### 2. Shelf Space Calculator
- **Functionality**: Calculate available and required shelf space.
- **Inputs**: Dimensions of shelf, bottle types.
- **Outputs**: Optimal arrangement, capacity status.
