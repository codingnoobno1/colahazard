# GST Compliance Rules & Knowledge Base

## 1. Registration Thresholds
*   **Goods**: ₹40 Lakhs (Annual Turnover)
*   **Services**: ₹20 Lakhs
*   **Special Category States**: ₹10 Lakhs

## 2. Invoicing Rules (Rule 46)
A tax invoice must contain:
1.  Name, address, and GSTIN of the supplier.
2.  Consecutive serial number (unique for a financial year).
3.  Date of issue.
4.  Name, address, and GSTIN of the recipient (if registered).
5.  HSN/SAC code (based on turnover).
6.  Description of goods/services.
7.  Total value and Taxable value.
8.  Rate of tax to be broken down (CGST, SGST, IGST).

## 3. Input Tax Credit (ITC)
*   **Eligibility**: Can check ITC only if the tax has been paid to the government by the supplier.
*   **Timeline**: ITC must be claimed by 30th November of the following financial year.
*   **Blocked Credits (Section 17(5))**: Motor vehicles (exceptions apply), food/beverages, beauty treatment, membership of clubs.

## 4. E-Way Bill
Required for movement of goods exceeding ₹50,000 in value.
*   **Part A**: Invoice details.
*   **Part B**: Transporter details.
